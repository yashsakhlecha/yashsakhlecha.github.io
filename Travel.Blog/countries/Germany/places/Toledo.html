Toledo






The capital of Spain before Madrid, it is a must visit place if you are looking for one day trips from Madrid.

How to get there?
Toledo is one of the farthest stops that can be reached by the local Madrid buses using just the transport card. For Toledo, the bus runs every 20 minutes from the metro station Plaza Eliptica.

Things to do here:

The city of Toledo is situated on a hilltop and has a very authentic feel to it. Although, the cathedral at Toledo is considered to be worth visiting and comes along with a 10 euro entry fee, we thought better

Next up on our itinerary was the Alcazar museum which is just massive and showcases the complete evolution and history of the Spanish military. The museum is built inside the Fort of Toledo and can be visited for free using a student id-card. Sipping a cup of coffee while gazing at the picturesque skyline from the top floor of this fort which also is the highest point of Toledo, is highly recommended.

On our way back down the hill, we took a small detour that lead took us through an even more beautiful path with scenes that seemed to be right off the movie screens!

Total Cost: 1 euro for the coffee :P
