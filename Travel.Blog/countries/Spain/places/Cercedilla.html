Camino de Agua, Cercedilla













At Cercedilla, there are multiple trails details about which can be inquired at the information desk at the base of this mountain. The staff is very friendly & helpful, however, language would definitely be an issue. Do remember to collect the Free map from here.

Cercedilla can be accessed via bus or train, the stops for which are approximately 1km from the information center.

Camino de Agua is the blue trail and one of the easiest treks around Madrid. The entire trail is very well marked and easy to follow. The path goes through a dense cover of trees and has a clean, fast flowing river going alongside for the initial part of the trek. The trail ends very close to the starting point from where you can easily trace your way back.

PS: On your way back, close to the bus stop, there is a tea shop filled with aromas from the lovely tea flavors present there. The owner is a big fan of India and its culture and you can stop by here for a friendly conversation.
