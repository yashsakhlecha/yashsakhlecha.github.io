Alcalá de Henares












Alcalá de Henares is a small but beautiful city located alongside the river Henares on the north eastern side of Madrid approximately 30kms away.

How to get there?
Alcalá de Henares can be easily reached by the local Madrid bus service. The bus runs on the line 223 and starts from the metro station Avenida América. Using the transport card, one can travel for free!

Things to do here:
Walking around this lovely city is a wonderful feeling in itself. The main attraction of this Alcala is the city center, which indeed is beautiful! One of the main features here is the pathway created by the tree trunks and it's branches that runs along both the sides of the city center square. It can be seen in the cover photo for this place.

There also is a huge church present in this Spanish city and we were lucky to witness one of their 'confirmation' ceremonies here. In this ceremony, the bishop bapitizes the local children in their early adolecence.

Taking a 15 minutes walk further into the city, one will arrive at the more residential part of town having a picturesque walking path having vast greenlands on one side and the Henares river and hills on the other. Although this isn't the main attraction here, for me it was the highlight of our visit.
